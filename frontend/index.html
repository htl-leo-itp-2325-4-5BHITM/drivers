What is DrivUs?

With DrivUs, we want to help everyone, whether parents, young people or senior citizens, to get to their destination comfortably and easily.
Our app is based on our users who offer rides for others and thus aims to achieve a balanced give and take.
With Drivus you can save money, make new friends and also contribute to climate protection.
We want to offer you these benefits and attract a large number of DrivUsers, which is why we are working on the best possible implementation of our app.
